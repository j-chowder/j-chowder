03

DataFest 2026: Best Visualization Award

Environmental barrier analysis for Stormont Vail Health (UCLA DataFest 2026). Engineered a composite social barrier index from UW County Health Rankings, mapped ICD-10 diagnoses to the Elixhauser Comorbidity Scale, and quantified ED follow-up gaps across social barrier types. Uncovered that housing instability and transportation need drive the largest care gaps, with mental health diagnoses clustering at highest risk — leading to a recommendation for barrier-triggered ED discharge navigation.

10 datasets social barriers 22% care gap
